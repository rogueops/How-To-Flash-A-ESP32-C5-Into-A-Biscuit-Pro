# How-To-Flash-A-ESP32-C5-Into-A-Biscuit-Pro
Step by step on how to flash a DIY ESP32-C5 board with the biscuit pro firmware!
Open Web Flasher: https://codehedge.github.io/diy_node_web_flasher/

Click on “Connect”.

Next step is Connecting your device to your PC.

a.    Press the BOOT button while connecting to your device.

b.    You will see your device appear, and show the (COM) port

Click on “Erase” this will clear anything that maybe on the device. (MAKE SURE YOU CHOOSE THE CORRECT PORT!) -unplug from the device and repeat 1. a.

Now, click in the box that says “SELECT BOARD” choose your device option

a.    I’m using a ESP32-C5, so I chose “Biscuit DIY Dev (ESP32-C5)

b.     Click on “VERSION”  and choose “Current”

c.    Click on “Program”

This will take at least 2 minutes or longer. “DO NOT UNPLUG YOUR DEVICE” 

When you see the words “FLASHING PROCESS COMPLERED!” In green, you can unplug it.  Its OK, go ahead and unplug it.

Plug it into the USB-C or if you already have power.

Open the Biscuit Manager on your phone and tap on “Connect to Biscuit”

Look for “C5-Dev Biscuit” when you see it, tap on it.

You are now ready to have fun.
